Firstly - who are we?

-Well this discord started as the four of us founders needing a place to hang out whilst we played Valorant but quickly we gained more mates to enjoy our time with. We have become a small community of game advocates,
honestly this server has a space for anyone and everyone - we only promote unity here! So just join a VC and introduce yourself, we don't bite!

  • Ranks and Regs

    • -We use a fluid rank system on this discord, the majority of users
    have access to all the good bits free of charge but its helpful to know our hierarchy.

  • User majority

    • -This is your Member, Chicken League, (To be continued) roles. We use these roles for organizing who's interested in playing what.
    Remember to use the react roles (when created) to make sure you have access to any chats or voice channels you may need.

  • Server Boosters and Community Champions

    • -We are all about rewarding the good efforts of the community so SB's and CC's have private chats and channels to use with your close friends.
    Community Champions are elected on a merit so stay as active as you can - its anyone's game!

  • Moderator roles

    • -Moderators are only promoted when our server needs them. We are against the over-policing of users and hold a "Use at your own discretion" policy. This essentially requires our moderators to use their common sense on whats tolerated and whats not.
    If you feel you have been wronged - DM another mod or founder for peer review. Moderators can be identified with a green name however we encourage mods to focus on integrating within the community.
